Documentation 📚

Social Sign provides an extra layer of verification to every Tweet you see on Twitter.

Before posting your Tweet, please click the new ink pen button in the Tweet menu bar to copy your digital signature to your clipboard. Paste the copied text to the end of your Tweet message.
